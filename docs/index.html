How It Works

Build Process

Your website is converted into a fully signed Android APK in minutes.

1

Validate Website

We check your URL is accessible and ready for conversion.

2

Generate Android Project

Capacitor creates a native Android project from your web app.

3

Configure WebView

Custom WebView with navigation, pull-to-refresh, and more.

4

Build APK

Gradle compiles your app into a release-ready APK.

5

Sign APK

Your APK is signed with a secure keystore for installation.

6

Generate Download Link

Your signed APK and AAB are ready to download.
